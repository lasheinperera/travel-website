ABOUT US

The blue waters of our resident landscape have served as an inspiration to many. Be it as a calming escape, a lively respite, or an unexplored world, the sea is not only an integral element of Sri Lanka's enchantment, but also of our home filled with coastal charms that extend an invitation to you. Born out of the historic splendour of Jetwing Seashells from 1978, our home has since grown into a symbol of modern elegance among Sri Lankan beach resorts. The enchantment of this nostalgic coastline has stayed true to our legendary family values. We look forward to sharing this magic with you at our home.

LOCATION

Resting upon one of the finest beaches Sri Lanka has to offer with an eternal summer and ever-present ocean breeze of the Indian Ocean, Crowny Sea is a modern Negombo hotel inspired by the vast emerald waters that give us our name. Located on the bustling coastal strip of Porutota Road, our home of Sri Lankan hospitality is in close proximity to a number of tropical shopping, exquisite fine dining, and adventurous Sri Lankan experiences that are bound to have you in high spirits throughout your holiday. As an added convenience, Jetwing Sea is one of our many hotels near the Bandaranaike International Airport. An ideal start or luxurious end to your adventures on our teardrop-shaped isle, Jetwing Sea remains easily accessible from a range of popular destinations across Sri Lanka, including our capital city of Colombo which can be reached in a brisk 30 minutes via the scenic Katunayake Expressway.

ACCOMADATION

Proudly welcoming you to our premier Sri Lankan beach resort, Negombo hosts Jetwing Sea upon the nostalgic shores where our legendary hospitality was born. With a total of 83 luxurious rooms, we invite you to indulge in the elegant comforts of our seaside home on your next coastal getaway. Jetwing Sea features three tiers of rooms spread across the four floors that look over the Indian Ocean. At the top, two exclusive Suites are reserved for those seeking spacious opulence with the lavish pleasures of a king-sized four-posted bed; separate sitting area, and a Jacuzzi bathtub for sumptuous soaks above the sea. Just below, 53 Deluxe Rooms enjoy an equal amount of coastal sunshine with expansive private balconies for afternoon lounging at our home of Sri Lankan hospitality. As a unique pleasure of our hospitality, both Suites and Deluxe Rooms also offer a magnificent view of the sea from their plush bathtubs.

FACILITIES

Complementing the luxury accommodation at Jetwing Sea, our beach hotel offers a holistic experience through a variety of dining options, wellness facilities, recreational activities, and other modern facilities and services for a carefree stay at our home of Sri Lankan hospitality in Negombo. With 24-hour room service available throughout the property, our associates at Jetwing Sea are prepared to serve you at any given point in time. The added pleasures of daily housekeeping and a dedicated laundry/dry cleaning service are also available for you to make use of, while babysitting services can also be arranged on request. To cater to your active lifestyle, our home of Sri Lankan hospitality features a dedicated area for beach volleyball upon our shores, a fully equipped gym with modern fitness equipment, and the luxury of having two swimming pools to choose from. In keeping with our commitment to holistic wellness experiences, Jetwing Sea also hosts a dedicated Reflexology Bar for quick treatments, as well as the Sky Spa - a blissful rooftop haven with professional therapists who offer a range of soothing therapies and treatment packs using natural ingredients that refresh and rejuvenate your mind, body, and soul.
